Potions

Our first NFT drop consists of a trading cards collection made up of 10+1 potions available on the Ethereum blockchain. Each potion card has a specific effect and intensity (that varies from 1 to a maximum of 5 drops).
With every purchase, every wizard and witch will receive a special NFT. If you are curious to know more, have a look at our roadmap!

Roadmap

We have many potions and experiments brewing in our cauldron and we decided to publish them following the percentage plan below. We will pass from one step to another every time the percentage of potions traded is reached.

20% - We will release new golden cards.

50% - We will release our first *magic* merchandise to thank our loyal witches and wizards.

60% - We will release our *magic* game!

80% - A collection of computer generated witches and wizards will be released so that every potions' owner will have its terrific avatar.

100% - Once this Roadmap is completed, we will start our second Roadmap with new tricks and magics for the community!
